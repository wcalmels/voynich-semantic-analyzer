# Support the Research

Voynich Semantic Analyzer is developed independently as part of an open computational humanities initiative focused on:

- medieval semantic corpora
- historical NLP
- semantic embeddings
- transformer-based semantic analysis
- digital humanities infrastructure

Support helps maintain:

- corpus infrastructure
- OCR processing
- semantic embedding computation
- hosting
- open research tooling
- medieval corpus preservation
